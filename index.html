Hi there.

I'm Jodi Parker, a Seattle-based designer and front end developer. I enjoy rock climbing, traveling, sketching, watercolor, listening to stories, drinking tea, and being outside.

I've done product design at EdgeTheory, editorial production at ARCADE magazine, marketing at Chadhaus, research and advocacy at Fairfood International, and freelance web design.

I hold a B.Sc. in Environmental Science, an A.A. in Liberal Arts & Sciences, and certificates in Wilderness Basics and Wildlife Handling.
