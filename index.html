I'm Jodi Mack, a Seattle-based designer. I enjoy rock climbing, traveling, listening to stories, riding my bike, drinking tea, and being outside.

I'm currently doing product design at AnswerDash. I've done UX design at PicMonkey, product design at EdgeTheory, editorial production at ARCADE magazine, and freelance web design.

I hold a B.Sc. in Environmental Science, an A.A. in Liberal Arts & Sciences, and certificates in Wilderness Basics and Wildlife Handling.
