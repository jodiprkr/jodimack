Hi there.

I'm Jodi Parker, a Seattle-based designer and developer. I fill my time with rock climbing, mindfulness, traveling, listening to stories, strong coffee, and being outside.

I've had the pleasure of doing research and advocacy at Fairfood International, editorial production at ARCADE magazine, and marketing for Chadhaus furniture design.

Currently, I'm expanding into UX design and looking to join a team that is woven with creativity, respect, and trust.
