Hi there

I’m Jodi Parker, a Seattle-based designer and developer.

Aside from design, I enjoy rock climbing, mindfulness, traveling, listening to stories, strong coffee, and being outside.

I've had the pleasure of doing research and advocacy at Fairfood International, editorial production at ARCADE magazine, and marketing for Chadhaus furniture design.

Currently, I'm expanding into UX design and looking to join a team that is woven with creativity, respect, and trust.

Fainting Goat Gelato

Fainting Goat Gelato was the first website I had the pleasure of launching. I noticed they only had a Blogspot as a website, and approached the owners about building a site for no charge. After convincing them that I had no ulterior motives, I was given full creative control with plenty of time to spread my wings.

Yoroshiku

I was approached by restaurant owner and friend, Kay Keisuke, about redesigning his previous website. Already a neighborhood favorite, I was thrilled to be a part of the big changes happening at Yoroshiku. This was a fun yet challenging project that allowed me to take my knowledge of web design a step further.
